This Privacy Policy (Policy) applies to your personal data and any other information that you may reveal or that DeafMatrix may collect whenever you engage with the App or our site. DeafMatrix shall endeavor to make all information that it collects secure and this Privacy Policy shall thus govern the collection, usage, sharing and storing of any information it gathers for its legitimate use. Personal information as used in this policy means the information relating to your device, posts, preferences and profile information.

This site/App may employ the use of Cookies, Log files, and Web beacons, tags and pixels to collect information on viewer traffic.

1. Information DeafMatrix Collects

1.1 DeafMatrix collects all information that is associated with your account and use of our services that include your profile information, Facebook account’s information where you register to our services using your Facebook account, your preferences and device information.

1.2 The information we collect may be grouped into three different categories, that is, Information that You Give Out, Information We Get from Other Sources, and Information Generated during Your Usage of our services.

1.3 During the registration of an account with us you will feed in personal information relating to your identity, personality, sexual preferences, gender, religion, ethnicity, tribe, race and related information. This information will be collected and stored in our system.

1.4 You may equally give similar information whenever you participate in our surveys, promotions, contests, focus groups, responses, events or other services that require you to give out personal information that will be stored in our servers.

1.5 Your information will also be collected whenever you engage our support or customer care department, whether to complain about other users or on personal initiative. Additionally, your chats with other users, your posts and contents that you post will be collected and stored by us.

1.6 Other users may contact DeafMatrix about your profile and may supply some information about you that will be stored by us. Besides, by logging in using your Facebook account, you will be sharing your Facebook account’s information including your login details.

1.7 Lastly, DeafMatrix will collect data relating to your use of our services including the pages you access, links, people you interact with, searches, clicks, date and time you logged in, duration and related data. DeafMatrix will equally collect your device information including cookie settings, wireless access points and names, IP address, browser type, version and language, app crashes, operating systems and time zone. You may also permit DeafMatrix to collect information related to your location information through various means such as Bluetooth, GPS or Wi-Fi connections.

2. Use of Cookies

2.1 DeafMatrix shall use cookies and other technologies to be able to recognize you and the device you are using. Such use shall be primarily for our legitimate business interest in ensuring that our services are available to you. In particular, the cookies will help to authenticate you, retain your settings and preferences, analyze traffic and trends as well as gauge the effectiveness of advertisement campaigns.

3.1 DeafMatrix shall use your information to deliver its services to you, to enforce its legal rights, to ensure your safety and any other legitimate purpose including running of advertisements. 3.2 Your information such as personal information will be used to administer your account by creating and managing your account, providing customer support and communicating our services to you.

3.3 We will use your profile information such as preferences to help match you and recommend you to other users as well as display your profile to others and vice versa.

3.4 The cookies data and device data that we collect will be used to help authenticate your account; allow consistency of our services whenever you use different devices or browsers, and enhance the efficiency of our services to you.

3.5 We may use the information we gather to help in developing, displaying and tracking content and run advertisement based on your preferences.

3.6 The information we gather on your usage of our services will be used for research and analysis on users’ behavior to help us to improve the services we offer and our content or features.

3.7 We shall equally collect and retain your data to help in the prevention, detection and fighting fraud, crime or other related illegal and unlawful conduct.

3.8 DeafMatrix will also use your information in ensuring that we comply with the requirements of the governing laws, help law enforcers or to enforce and/or exercise our rights.

4.1 DeafMatrix will not share your information unless it is intended to aid your use of our services or for other legitimate interests and legal purposes. We may therefore share your information with other users to aid in connection, with service providers and legal authorities.

We however encourage you to exercise care and caution on the kind of information that you make available on your profile since the same is subject to abuse and misuse by other users.

4.3 DeafMatrix may from time to time enlist third-party service providers to ensure that we deliver to your satisfaction. This may include those we engage to host and manage our data, advertising, analytics, customer care, payment processing, marketing, and security operations. 11.1 DeafMatrix will take all reasonable precautions and follow what we believe are commercially reasonable practices to ensure the safety of your personal information. 12.1 DeafMatrix shall retain your data only as long as it is relevant for its legitimate interests and as is authorized under the applicable law. During this period, no user may access the information on our services.

12.2 We will delete or anonymize your information after the safety period of three months unless:

(i) The law requires it to be kept as is the case with traffic data which may be kept for up to one year to comply with statutory obligations on data retention;

(ii) Where the data is kept to act as evidence of our compliance with the law such as records of consents to our Terms, Privacy Policy and related consents that are kept for five years;

(iii) There are issues that are outstanding or claim or dispute that require the said data to be kept until the issue or claim is resolved; or

(iv) The information is kept for our legitimate business interest including the prevention of fraud, ensuring safety of users and the security of our systems.
